My Book Shelf




👋 🙏 🙋 So that the discipline and designer themselves don't become bigger than people and problem, these are my go to 📚. 🌃 is the limit, design is interdisciplinary, let's read about culture. Also, you can ✍ me recommendations here 📱. Let's 💬 !

Design as Art

Bruno Munari

“re-establishes the long-lost contact between art and the public”

Munari was described by Picasso as 'the new Leonardo'.

Ways of Seeing

John Berger

“All publicity works upon anxiety.”

If you can watch Berger's BBC show as well. Also Tilda Swinton made a documentary on him.

Design of everyday things

Don Norman

“Cognition attempts to make sense of the world: emotion assigns value.”

Okay! This doesn't need description.

Culture and Imperialism

Edward Said

“No one today is purely one thing”

Just google Said.

Making and breaking the grid

Timothy Samara

Absolute fundamental.

Thinking with type

Ellen Lupton

“Typography is what language looks like.”

Of course!

Interaction of Colours

Josef Albers

Have you ever paid attention to a painting? Colors merging into each other, their relative presence creating effects of things. This is book is about that.

Information Anxiety

Richard Saul Wurman

“We live in an age of alsos, adapting to alternatives”

A must read for a designer.
