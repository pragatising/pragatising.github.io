About

I'm a User Experience Designer and a recent graduate of Georgia Tech' MS - HCI program. I care for thoughtfully crafted solutions with an emphasis on critical inquiry, ethics and social impact. I am highly observant, curious, passionate and experimental in my work.

My process is informed by my training in user-centred design, love for interaction and visual design, comprehensive knowledge of UX methodologies and holistic systems thinking. I am constantly learning about human behaviour and psychology to inform my design choices and ensure scientific crafting of solutions.

When I am not designing, I am either making digital illustrations for storytelling ( Check out my instagram! ) or learning about social, political, philosophical niceties of design processes, artefacts and methodologies.
